# Orcestr Community Token

ORCESTR is planned as an experimental Community Support Token for Orcestr and its author [@Artasov](https://github.com/Artasov).

Orcestr is a personal product-codebase evolving into a public developer toolkit and open-source library. The token is not the center of the project. It is a possible community layer for people who want to follow and support this work early.

## Name

Name: Orcestr

Ticker: ORCESTR

## Purpose

The token may be used for:

- early supporter identity;
- supporter badges;
- optional beta access when public testing opens;
- private or early development updates;
- community visibility around the Orcestr build;
- small non-financial perks where legally and operationally safe.

The token is not required to use Orcestr products. Future paid product flows should not depend on token ownership.

## Product Relationship

Orcestr remains product-first. The token can support the community around the work, but it does not replace the product, the roadmap or the business model.

Product development should remain valuable even if the token has no market value.

## What The Token Does Not Provide

ORCESTR does not provide:

- equity in Orcestr;
- revenue share;
- guaranteed financial return;
- guaranteed price growth;
- governance over company decisions;
- guaranteed AI credits or free product usage.

AI generation has real provider costs, so credits and product access must stay economically sustainable.

## Possible Holder Perks

Possible perks may include:

- supporter badge;
- early beta access when public testing opens;
- private development updates;
- Telegram or community role;
- optional supporter wall for users who opt in;
- non-critical roadmap preference votes.

Any perks are experimental, limited and can change. They should not create an expectation of profit.

## Risk

ORCESTR is experimental. It may never receive broad utility, may have limited liquidity and may lose all market value.

Do not treat it as an investment, equity, revenue share or a promise of future profit.
